Image guidelines:
- The image must clearly show the SCENARIO SETUP — what the child sees and must respond to.
- For social/question scenarios: show people in a relatable situation with clear body language and context.
- For vocabulary/word scenarios: show the concept visually (not just the word label).
- For emotion scenarios: show a face or body with the emotion clearly displayed.
- Simple, uncluttered backgrounds. Friendly cartoon style. Not photorealistic.
- No text overlays in the image — text is shown separately in the app.
- Diverse characters when people are relevant. Calm colors. Avoid visual overwhelm.
